SCP-001

SCP-001 is one of the most important slots on the site, because to many it represents the “origin” of the Foundation. It is so important, in fact, that there is no definite SCP-001 on the site. Instead, there is a list of SCP-001 Proposals, made by many different authors. In-universe, the explanation for this is that the different SCP-001 articles serve as a smokescreen for each other; there is only one “real” SCP-001, with all of the others serving just as cover-ups and fakes: any one of the proposals could be the "real" 001, or none of them could be. In reality, the slot was deemed too important to be set to only one article, and so authors are free to make their own SCP-001 proposals.

Most 001 Proposals will have something to do with the in-universe origin of the Foundation, with some of them explicitly telling stories of how it came to be, but others may instead have something to do with the “end” of it. Format screws and long articles are common among proposals, with some resembling tales more than actual articles.

A list of all SCP-001 Proposals can be found here. Careful for the memetic kill agent.
