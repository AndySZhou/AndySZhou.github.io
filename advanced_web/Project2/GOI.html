Groups of Interest

Groups of Interest, or GoIs, are groups that deal with anomalous objects other than the Foundation that exist in the same universe. Each group has its own philosophy regarding anomalous object, most of which conflict with or are in direct opposition of the Foundation’s goals. A list of all GoIs can be found here, but some of the most notable GoIs include:

GOC Hub



As opposed to the Foundation’s policy of containing anomalies, the Global Occult Coalition, or GOC, is much more interested in destroying them. Much more militaristic than the Foundation. The GOC and the Foundation have a tentative policy of non-interference with each other, and in some cases even collaborate.



Chaos Insurgency Hub



The Insurgency is a group that split off of the Foundation due to differences in philosophy regarding anomalies. Whereas the Foundation has a strict policy of containment, the Chaos Insurgency would rather use and weaponize anomalies.



Serpent's Hand Hub



The Serpent’s Hand believes in the preservation and normalization of the anomalous, and are in direct opposition to both the GOC and the Foundation. They are associated with an anomalous place known as the Wanderer’s Library. There is also a sister site to the SCP Wiki called The Wanderer’s Library, with more of a focus on fantastical elements and inspiring a sense of wonder, as opposed to the SCP Wiki’s focus on horror and sci-fi.



MC&D Hub

MC&D focuses on the sale and trade of anomalous items, and is generally a neutral organization, having dealings with both the Foundation and other GoIs. Deals mostly with the super-rich. The Marshall, Carter, and Dark Hub can be found here.
