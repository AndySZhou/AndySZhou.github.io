Introduction

The SCP Wiki is a website based around a fictional organization called the SCP (Secure, Contain, Protect) Foundation, with over 4000 articles written by various different authors. With a strong leaning towards horror and sci-fi, it is comprised of some of the most creative and unique writing on the Internet, but it’s unique format can mean that it’s hard to understand and get into for some.

Just going in and reading articles, there are several aspects of the universe that will go unexplained unless the viewer read pages or supplementary background text, which can hamper the experience. So instead of people having to go find and read those pages of text (most of which can be found here), I’m making this guide so that they can read all of these pages of text instead.

Content Warning:

The SCP Wiki is a horror-themed site about an organization that uses sometimes questionable ethics in service of the greater good. As such, some articles will involve subject matter that is difficult for some people to read about and the wiki should be read at the reader’s own discretion.
