Miscellaneous

This guide is mostly meant as an introduction to reading SCPs, but it’s important to remember that there is an entire community surrounding it. There are numerous guidelines for commenting on articles, writing SCPs, site etiquette, and other facets of the community which can be found here.

Additionally, if you’re ever having trouble understanding or reading an SCP, maybe because it’s too long, too meta, or too filled with jargon, you can check out the SCP Declassified Subreddit. Here, various authors post threads dedicated to picking apart some of the harder-to-understand articles piece by piece to make them as easy to read as possible. Some of these declasses are also in video form, and there are various Youtubers who also base their videos around a similar concept. A page with all of the currently declassified SCPs can be found here.

The SCP Wiki is also translated into several other languages, each with their own site. The hub those sites can be found here.
